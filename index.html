Main Program

Keynote speeches

Keynote speeches from experts in the VR industry can provide attendees with valuable insights into the latest trends, innovations, and challenges in the field.

Panel discussions

Panel discussions featuring experts in various aspects of VR can provide attendees with a deeper understanding of specific topics and allow them to hear different perspectives and insights.

Workshops and training sessions can provide attendees with hands-on experience with VR technology and allow them to develop practical skills and knowledge.

VR experiences and demonstrations can give attendees the opportunity to try out the latest VR hardware and software and experience immersive and interactive virtual environments.
